Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025 - 28 - 09

This privacy policy describes how Rise Sync ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Rise Sync Apple Watch app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Usage data: app usage, crash reports, analytics (may be collected via third-party services).
  • Health & sensor data: only if your app collects it — e.g., heart rate, steps. We request explicit permission from HealthKit/HealthKit data sources and only use it for the app's core features.
  • Device information: device model, OS version, and identifiers used for debugging and analytics.

2. How We Use Data

  • Provide and operate the app’s features.
  • Improve app performance and user experience (analytics, crash reporting).
  • Debugging and support.

3. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. We may share limited data with third-party services for analytics and crash reporting (for example: Firebase, Sentry, Analytics providers). These providers are bound by their own privacy policies.

4. Health Data

If the app accesses Health data (HealthKit), we only read the data you explicitly grant access to and we use it to provide the health-related features of the app. We do not share Health data with third parties except as required for the app's functionality and only with your consent.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage is fully secure.

6. Retention

We retain data only as long as necessary to provide the service and for analytics purposes in anonymised/aggregated form.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal data we hold about you. Contact us at: developer@happyventures.com.au

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The "Last updated" date will reflect changes.
